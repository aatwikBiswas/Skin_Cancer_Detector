05About the ML Model

🧠

Architecture

A Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) based on EfficientNet-B3, fine-tuned for dermoscopic lesion classification with data augmentation and transfer learning.

📦

Dataset — HAM10000

Trained on the Human Against Machine (HAM10000) dataset: 10,015 dermoscopic images spanning 7 lesion classes including melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, and benign keratosis.

📊

Performance

Achieves ~91% top-1 accuracy on the validation set. AUC-ROC of 0.96 for melanoma vs. benign classification. Evaluated on a balanced hold-out set.

🔒

Privacy

All analysis runs client-side in this demo. No images are uploaded to a server. In production, HIPAA-compliant encrypted pipelines would be used.
